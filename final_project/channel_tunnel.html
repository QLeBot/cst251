The English Channel Tunnel

The Chunnel

Plans started in 1802, the actual construction began in 1988 and finished in 1994. The tunnel that goes under the English Channel between the UK and France
connects the two contries through the long tunnel that took 6 years to build. Built by tunneling from both sides with machines that met half way.
After finishing the tunneling, both machines were left half way and buried during the adding of cement.

The Wonder

Designated a Modern Wonder of the World in 1994 by the American Society of Civil Engineers, it has been accepted as a wonder for being the longest and deepest
undersea tunnel. It goes under the sea 38km (23.6 miles) and 50 meters under the sea bed of the English Channel.
It is expected to stay as a key connection between the two Countries for years to come.
