The evolution of the World's Wonders

From antiquity, a list of the world's most spectacular natural features and human-built structures have been compiled.
The Seven Wonders of the Ancient World is the oldest known list, documenting the most remarkable man-made creations of classical antiquity.
Along the years this list evolved, changed to accomodate new discoveries, new civilizations and the state of the previous Wonders.
Based on this, we wanted to express each our vision of today's World's Wonders. This website is compiling our ideas of what is most remarkable in our modern world.
