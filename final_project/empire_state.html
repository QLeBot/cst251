The Empire State Building

The Building

The construction of the Empire State Building began in the 1930s with a goal to be the worlds tallest building of its time. Completed a year later,
the Empire State Building held the title of tallest building until 1970 when the Wolrd Trade Center was constructed.

The Wonder

Designated a Wonder by the American Society of Civil Engineers, it has been accepted as one of the seven Modern Wonders of the World due to breaking the record
for having more than 100 floors and being an impressive feat to achieve in its time. It has been seen as a symbol of New York City
throughout multiple films like King Kong and James and the Giant Peach.
